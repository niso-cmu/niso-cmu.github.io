# About NISO

## Mission & Vision

<!-- EDIT ME: replace with the mission/vision statement from

NISO (Neuroscience Institute Student Organization) exists to build community among Masters and
PhD students in the CMU Neuroscience Institute, connect students to resources across the
Institute and CNBC, and organize outreach, academic, and social programming.
